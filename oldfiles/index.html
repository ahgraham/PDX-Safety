Poliapp

This is a project by Wilson High School students to get police officers and the community working together.

The police puts out alerts, which you can view by:

  1. Clicking the menu button or swipe to get to the menu
  2. Clicking on districts
  3. Clicking on the districts you want alerts from
  4. Going back to the menu and clicking "Alerts"

If you want to see all the Alerts, unset/don't set your settings.
